## 2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a secure, functional, and personalized experience. The categories of data we collect are as follows.

### 2.1 Personal Information

When you register and use CUI CHAT, we collect:

- **Full Name** — used to display your identity to other users within the app.
- **Email Address** — used for account registration, authentication, and account recovery.
- **Profile Photo** — uploaded voluntarily by you to personalize your profile.
- **University Registration Details** (where applicable) — used to verify your identity as a member of the COMSATS University Islamabad community.

### 2.2 User-Generated Content

When you use the app's core features, we collect and store:

- **Chat Messages** — text messages, images, and video files exchanged in private or group conversations.
- **Stories and Reels** — images and short video clips uploaded by you to your story or reel feed.
- **Posts and Comments** — text, images, audio, and video content you publish on your profile feed, along with associated comments and reactions.
- **Media Files** — any files (photos, videos, audio recordings) you upload through the app.

### 2.3 Device and Technical Data

To deliver push notifications and maintain service reliability, we collect:

- **FCM Device Token** — a unique identifier generated by Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver push notifications to your device.
- **Device Type** — whether your device runs Android or iOS, used to format notifications correctly.
- **IP Address** — collected automatically by our servers to facilitate network communication and detect abuse.
- **Basic Device Specifications** — including operating system version and device model, used for debugging and compatibility.

### 2.4 Usage Data

We may collect information about how you interact with the App, including:

- Features accessed and frequency of use.
- App crash reports and performance diagnostics.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **To provide and operate the App** — enabling real-time messaging, story sharing, reel publishing, room-based group interactions, and social features.
- **To send push notifications** — alerting you to new messages, chat requests, follows, likes, comments, and other activity relevant to your account.
- **To verify your identity** — confirming your university affiliation during registration where required.
- **To personalize your experience** — displaying your name, photo, and content to other users.
- **To maintain security and prevent abuse** — detecting and responding to fraudulent, unauthorized, or illegal activity.
- **To improve the App** — analyzing usage patterns and crash reports to fix bugs and enhance performance.
- **To comply with legal obligations** — responding to lawful requests from regulatory authorities where required.

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

## 4. Real-Time Processing and Data Storage

CUI CHAT operates a hybrid data architecture:

- **Real-Time Messaging:** All chat messages and conversation data are processed and stored in real time using **Google Firebase Firestore**, a secure, cloud-hosted NoSQL database maintained by Google.
- **Application Data:** User profiles, posts, stories, reels, and media files are stored on secure cloud servers managed by our Laravel backend, hosted on **Hostinger** cloud infrastructure.
- **Media Files:** Uploaded images and videos are stored on our Hostinger-managed storage servers and served over HTTPS connections.

All servers used by CUI CHAT apply industry-standard access controls, and access to production data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

---

## 5. Data Security and Encryption

We take the security of your data seriously. The following measures are in place:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using **HTTPS/SSL (TLS)** protocols, preventing unauthorized interception of your data in transit.
- **Firebase Security Rules:** Access to Firestore data is governed by Firebase Security Rules that restrict read and write access to authenticated, authorized users only.
- **Authentication:** User sessions are managed through secure authentication tokens.
- **Server-Side Security:** Our Laravel backend enforces input validation, parameterized queries, and role-based access controls to prevent unauthorized data access.

While we implement strong security practices, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to protect your account credentials and notify us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized access to your account.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide you with the App's services.

- **Active Accounts:** Data is retained for the lifetime of your account.
- **Inactive Accounts:** If your account has been inactive for an extended period, we may delete it along with all associated data after providing reasonable notice.
- **Deleted Accounts:** Upon account deletion (see Section 7), all personal data associated with your account is permanently removed from our active systems within **30 days** of the deletion request. Residual copies in backup systems are purged within **90 days**.

---

## 7. Account Deletion and Data Removal

In compliance with Google Play Store policies and applicable privacy regulations, you have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time.

**How to delete your account:**

1. Open the CUI CHAT app.
2. Navigate to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**.
3. Confirm your deletion request.

Upon confirmation, the following data will be permanently deleted:

- Your profile information (name, email, profile photo).
- All messages sent and received in private conversations.
- All posts, stories, reels, and comments you have created.
- Your device token and notification preferences.
- All media files you have uploaded.

**Data deletion requests submitted via email:** You may also request account deletion by emailing us at **contact@cuichat.online** with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days.
