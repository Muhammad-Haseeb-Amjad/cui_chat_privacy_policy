1. Introduction

Welcome to CUI CHAT, a real-time messaging and social application developed and operated by the CUI CHAT Team (“we”, “us”, or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the CUI CHAT mobile application (“App”) available on the Google Play Store.

By downloading, installing, or using CUI CHAT, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the App immediately.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a secure, functional, and personalized experience.

2.1 Personal Information

  • Full Name — displayed to other users within the app.
  • Email Address — used for registration, authentication, and account recovery.
  • Profile Photo — uploaded voluntarily to personalize your profile.
  • University Registration Details (where applicable) — used to verify your identity as a member of COMSATS University Islamabad.

2.2 User-Generated Content

  • Chat Messages — text messages, images, and video files exchanged in private or group conversations.
  • Stories and Reels — images and short video clips uploaded to your story or reel feed.
  • Posts and Comments — text, images, audio, and video content published on your profile feed, along with associated comments and reactions.
  • Media Files — any files (photos, videos, audio recordings) you upload through the app.

2.3 Device and Technical Data

  • FCM Device Token — a unique identifier generated by Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver push notifications to your device.
  • Device Type — Android or iOS, used to format notifications correctly.
  • IP Address — collected automatically by our servers to facilitate communication and detect abuse.
  • Basic Device Specifications — OS version and device model, used for debugging and compatibility.

2.4 Usage Data

  • Features accessed and frequency of use.
  • App crash reports and performance diagnostics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App — enabling real-time messaging, story sharing, reel publishing, and social features.
  • To send push notifications — alerting you to new messages, follows, likes, comments, and other activity.
  • To verify your identity — confirming your university affiliation during registration.
  • To personalize your experience — displaying your name, photo, and content to other users.
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse — detecting and responding to unauthorized or illegal activity.
  • To improve the App — analyzing usage patterns and crash reports to fix bugs and enhance performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations — responding to lawful requests from regulatory authorities.
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Real-Time Processing and Data Storage

CUI CHAT operates a hybrid data architecture:

  • Real-Time Messaging: Chat messages and conversation data are processed and stored in real time using Google Firebase Firestore, a secure cloud-hosted NoSQL database maintained by Google.
  • Application Data: User profiles, posts, stories, reels, and media files are stored on secure cloud servers managed by our Laravel backend hosted on Hostinger cloud infrastructure.
  • Media Files: Uploaded images and videos are stored on our Hostinger-managed storage servers and served exclusively over HTTPS connections.

All servers apply industry-standard access controls. Access to production data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Data Security and Encryption

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL (TLS) protocols, preventing unauthorized interception.
  • Firebase Security Rules: Access to Firestore data is governed by Firebase Security Rules restricting read and write access to authenticated and authorized users only.
  • Authentication: User sessions are managed through secure authentication tokens.
  • Server-Side Security: Our Laravel backend enforces input validation, parameterized queries, and role-based access controls.
While we implement strong security practices, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Please protect your account credentials and notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide the App’s services.

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained for the lifetime of your account.
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for an extended period may be deleted after reasonable notice.
  • Deleted Accounts: All personal data is permanently removed from active systems within 30 days of deletion request. Residual backup copies are purged within 90 days.

7. Account Deletion and Data Removal

In compliance with Google Play Store policies and applicable privacy regulations, you have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time.

How to delete your account:

  1. Open the CUI CHAT app.
  2. Navigate to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your deletion request.

Upon confirmation, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your profile information (name, email, profile photo).
  • All messages sent and received in private conversations.
  • All posts, stories, reels, and comments you have created.
  • Your device token and notification preferences.
  • All media files you have uploaded.
You may also request account deletion by emailing contact@cuichat.online with the subject line “Account Deletion Request”. We will process your request within 30 days.

9. Children’s Privacy

CUI CHAT is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under the age of 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at contact@cuichat.online. We will promptly delete the relevant information from our records.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right of Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure — request deletion of your personal data (see Section 7).
  • Right to Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data in certain circumstances.
